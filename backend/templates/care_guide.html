Pet care guide

General tips for keeping your pets healthy and happy.

Dog care tips

  • Provide daily exercise such as walks or playtime to keep joints and muscles active.
  • Offer clean, fresh water at all times, especially after exercise.
  • Brush your dog's coat regularly to reduce shedding and check the skin.
  • Use vet-approved flea and tick prevention, especially in warm seasons.
  • Schedule routine veterinary checkups at least once a year.

Cat care tips

  • Provide scratching posts and vertical spaces to reduce stress and boredom.
  • Keep litter boxes clean and place them in quiet, safe locations.
  • Encourage play with toys that simulate hunting to keep cats active.
  • Keep cats indoors or supervise outdoor time to reduce the risk of injury.
  • Have regular vet checkups and dental examinations.

Horse care tips

  • Provide safe turnout space with shelter from sun, wind, and rain.
  • Ensure regular farrier visits to maintain healthy hooves.
  • Offer constant access to clean water and good-quality forage (hay or pasture).
  • Schedule routine deworming and vaccinations as recommended by your vet.

Rabbit care tips

  • House rabbits in a spacious, secure enclosure with room to hop and stand upright.
  • Provide hiding places and chew toys to reduce stress and wear down teeth.
  • Keep litter boxes and bedding clean and dry.
  • Check teeth, eyes, and bottom regularly for signs of overgrowth or infection.

Parrot / bird care tips

  • Use a cage that allows birds to fully stretch their wings and climb.
  • Provide perches of different widths and textures to protect feet.
  • Offer daily out-of-cage time in a safe, supervised room.
  • Keep the environment free from smoke, aerosols, and non-stick fumes.

Hamster care tips

  • Choose a cage with a solid base and plenty of floor space, not just tubes.
  • Provide deep bedding for burrowing and nesting.
  • Use an appropriately sized, solid running wheel to encourage exercise.
  • Handle gently and avoid waking hamsters abruptly from deep sleep.

Turtle care tips

  • Provide a basking area with UVB lighting and proper temperature gradient.
  • Keep water clean with a good filter and regular partial water changes.
  • Offer a species-appropriate mix of pellets, greens, and protein foods.
  • Monitor shell for soft spots, discoloration, or injury.

Fish care tips

  • Cycle the aquarium before adding fish to establish beneficial bacteria.
  • Test water parameters regularly for ammonia, nitrite, nitrate, and pH.
  • Avoid overfeeding; only feed what fish eat in a couple of minutes.
  • Quarantine new fish before adding them to the main tank when possible.

Diet by age

Puppies & kittens: multiple small meals per day using puppy/kitten-specific food.

Adults: 2 balanced meals per day, adjusting portions to maintain a healthy body shape.

Seniors: lower calorie, easy-to-digest diets and more frequent health checks.

Vaccination overview

Vaccinations help protect your pet from serious diseases. Your vet can build a schedule based on:

  • Your pet's age and previous vaccine history.
  • Risk of exposure to other animals or wildlife.
  • Local regulations, especially for rabies.
